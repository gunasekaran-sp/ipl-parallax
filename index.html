Teams

CSK: The Chennai Super Kings are a franchise cricket team based in Chennai. The team is being captained by Mahendra Singh Dhoni and coached by Stephen Fleming. The Super Kings have won the title twice in succession (2010 and 2011).They have been the runner-up team for 4 times.

Kings XI: The Kings XI Punjab are a franchise cricket team based in Ajitgarh, Punjab, that plays in the Indian Premier League. The team is jointly owned by Preity Zinta, Ness Wadia, Mohit Burman and Karan Paul. The team is captained by George Bailey.They have been the runner-up during the year 2014.

KKR: The Kolkata Knight Riders are a cricket franchise representing Kolkata in the Indian Premier League. The franchise is owned by Bollywood actor Shah Rukh Khan and Juhi Chawla.The team is been captained by Gautham Gambhir. They won the title twice(2012 & 2014).

SRH: The SunRisers Hyderabad are a cricket franchise based in the city of Hyderabad that plays in the Indian Premier League.The team is owned by Kalanithi Maran of the Sun TV channel Network. Krish Srikkanth and VVS Laxman are the mentors of the team.They have won the title once in the year.

RR: The Rajasthan Royals (often abbreviated as RR) are a cricket franchise representing the city of Jaipur in the Indian Premier League. The team is owned by Manoj Badale and is currently captained by Steve Smith. They won the winners title in the first season of IPL(2008).

RCB: The Royal Challengers Bangalore are a franchise cricket team based in Bengaluru, Karnataka that plays in the Indian Premier League. The team is owned by Vijay Mallya and the team is been captained by virat kohli and coached by Daniel Vettori. They have been the runner-up during the year 2009 and 2011.

DD: The Delhi Daredevils are a cricket franchise representing the city of Delhi in the IPL. The team's current captain is Jean-Paul Duminy while the coach is Gary Kirsten. The leading run-scorer of the side is Virender Sehwag while the leading wicket-taker is Irfan Pathan.

MI: The Mumbai Indians are a franchise cricket team representing the city of Mumbai in the Indian Premier League.The Mumbai Indians are currently captained by Rohit Sharma, and coached by Ricky Ponting.They won the winners title twice(2013 and 2015) and are runner-up once in the year 2010.
